# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in this
project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size,
disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and
orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the project.

Unacceptable behaviour includes harassment, insulting or derogatory comments,
publishing others' private information, and other conduct which could reasonably be
considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be
reported to the maintainers via @StarsCashFlowbot. All complaints will be reviewed
and investigated promptly and fairly. Maintainers are obligated to respect the
privacy and security of the reporter of any incident.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org),
version 2.1.
